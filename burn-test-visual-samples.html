Burn Test Protocol

Professional fiber identification through controlled burn analysis

The Process

3-Step Burn Test Procedure

1
Isolate Sample
Pull a small tuft from a hidden area (closet corner, under furniture). Use tweezers — never cut loop pile.
2
Ignite & Observe
Use odorless butane lighter (never matches). Hold tuft to flame. Watch for: melt vs. sizzle vs. burn.
3
Check 4 Pillars
Document: Flame behavior, Smoke color, Odor, and Residue (ash or bead).
