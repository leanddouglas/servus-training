About This Program

Master the Process. Protect the Asset.

The Servus Group Professional Carpet Care Certification is an industry-leading training program built on the IICRC S100 Standard for Professional Cleaning of Textile Floor Covering. Designed for the Servus Group Lower Mainland team, this program goes beyond basic instruction — combining fiber science, cleaning chemistry, advanced extraction methods, stain treatment protocols, and professional client management into one cohesive curriculum.

We operate on a core formula: EX (Employee Experience) + CX (Customer Experience) = Growth. By investing in your knowledge and safety, we ensure you deliver unparalleled, professional results to our clients.

What You'll Achieve

Core Competencies

Fiber Identification

Identify carpet fiber types on sight and choose safe cleaning methods for each material.

Chemistry Mastery

Understand pH, surfactants, enzymes, and oxidizers to select the right product every time.

Equipment Expertise

Operate HWE portables, truck-mounts, and Whittaker machines with confidence and precision.

Stain Treatment

Diagnose and treat any stain — from pet contamination to red wine — using the correct protocols.

Client Excellence

Communicate professionally, manage expectations, and build lasting client relationships.

Certified Professional

Pass the 50-question certification exam and earn your Servus Group professional credential.
